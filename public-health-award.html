SEAPHEIN Awards

Public Health Education Leadership Award for Institutions

The member institutions are contributing to promoting Public Health Education by developing curriculum and offering courses in public health discipline and domain areas. These institutions have supported the creation of human resources for managing public health, health policy planning, program management, and improving health systems performance using new technology and innovations. The following 22 member institutions are recognized for Public Health Education Leadership Award.
